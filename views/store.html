About Kid Reads


Our mission is to publish childrens books in a new format. By producing books in a new digital spectrum, a child can bring their entire library with them wherever they go!


Producing books in a mobile format has many uses.

  • Long car ride? Waiting in the doctors office? Your child's very first airplane ride? Any of those boring or stressful moments in life can now be filled with reading!
  • Teachers can now have iPad's in the classroom so children can read and explore books at their leisure.
  • Children with learning disabilities now can approach books with a full spectrum of exerience, tactile, auditory, and visual.
  • For children or adults where english is a second language, the Kid Reads format offers a very full reading experience.
